Common questions
How much can I borrow for a wedding?
Loan amounts depend on your personal circumstances and what you can comfortably repay. Apply online or contact us to discuss the amount you need.
Can I include the honeymoon in the loan?
Yes — a wedding loan can cover any wedding-related expenses including flights, accommodation, and honeymoon costs.
When should I apply?
We recommend applying well in advance of your wedding date — ideally 2–3 months before you need to make deposits or final payments to suppliers.
Can we apply as a couple?
Contact us to discuss joint applications. We will guide you through the process.
What if our circumstances change before the wedding?
Contact us as soon as possible if your circumstances change. Our hardship team can work with you if needed.
