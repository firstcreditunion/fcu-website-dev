Common questions
What can I use a travel loan for?
A travel loan can be used for flights, accommodation, travel packages, tours, travel insurance, or any other travel-related expense.
How much can I borrow for a travel loan?
Loan amounts depend on your personal circumstances and what you can comfortably repay. Apply online or contact us to discuss how much you need.
Can I combine a travel loan with my travel savings account?
Yes — many members save as much as they can in a Travel Account and use a loan to cover the remainder. This is a cost-effective approach that minimises the amount of interest you pay.
What if my trip gets cancelled?
Your loan obligations remain regardless of whether your trip proceeds. We recommend considering travel insurance to protect your plans. Contact us if you experience financial hardship.
How quickly can I get the funds?
Once your application is approved and documents are signed, funds are released to your account. The process from application to funding is typically 1–3 working days.
