Support

We’re here to help when things don’t go as planned

First Credit Union is a member-owned financial institution that strives to look after our members’ financial needs, even in difficult times. If you are struggling to meet your loan repayments, please contact us as soon as possible — the sooner we know, the more we can do to help.

Contact us now Email hardship team
What counts as financial hardship?

Illness or injury affecting your ability to work or earn income

A major life event such as the death of a family member

Relationship breakdown

Loss of employment or significantly reduced hours

Any other reasonable cause of unforeseen financial difficulty

If your situation is not listed, contact us — we assess each case individually

Options available to you

Use advance repayments

If you have made extra repayments ahead of schedule, these may be available to cover your repayments during a hardship period.

Extend your loan term

Extending the loan term reduces your regular repayments. Note that this will increase the total interest payable over the life of the loan.

Defer or reduce repayments

In some circumstances we may be able to defer or reduce your repayments temporarily while you get back on your feet.

Have loan protection insurance? If you have Loan Protection Insurance with us, your policy may cover your repayments during illness, injury, or redundancy. Check your cover →
How to apply for hardship assistance
1

Contact us

Call our team on our Call Centre number, visit a branch, or email hardship@firstcu.co.nz to start the process.

2

Provide information

We may ask for supporting documentation depending on the reason for your hardship, such as proof of loss of income or a medical certificate.

3

We work with you

Our team will review your situation and work with you on the best arrangement to help you manage through the difficult period.

Act early: The sooner you contact us, the more options we have available to help you. Please do not wait until you have missed payments before reaching out. Contact our hardship team at hardship@firstcu.co.nz.
Common questions
How do I apply for hardship assistance?
Contact us as soon as possible by calling our Call Centre (Monday 10am–5pm, Tuesday–Friday 8am–5pm), visiting a branch, or emailing hardship@firstcu.co.nz. We will guide you through the process.
What documentation will I need?
This depends on your situation. We may require proof of loss of income, a medical certificate, or other documentation. Our team will advise you on what is needed when you get in touch.
Will hardship affect my credit rating?
We encourage you to speak with us about your individual situation. Acting early and working with us proactively is always the best course of action.
What if my situation isn’t covered by the listed causes?
Contact our collection solutions team at hardship@firstcu.co.nz or visit your nearest branch. We assess every situation individually.
Do I need to have loan protection insurance to get hardship assistance?
No. Hardship assistance is available to all members regardless of whether they have loan protection insurance. Insurance may provide additional cover — check your policy details.
